Sons of Anarchy (Aired 2008-2014)

Rating: 8.7/10
Age Restriction: TV-MA

Jackson 'Jax' Teller is part of a Motorcycle Club created by his father and his father's best friend. When he finds and reads a manuscript his dad wrote about how he wanted the motorcycle club to turn out, he started to see things in a new light and set out to make the Motorcycle Club what his dad wanted it to be.
